How It Works

Use this page to explain the steps customers and tailors will follow when using the marketplace.

  1. Step 1: Customers browse tailor profiles and compare services.
  2. Step 2: They request a quote, fitting, or consultation.
  3. Step 3: Tailors confirm details, timing, and pricing.
  4. Step 4: Both sides track the order and final delivery.
